A Sera Guide to Where to Eat Around the World

If you plan your holidays around what you’re going to eat, you’re in the right place. This guide is built entirely from the Sera community’s own travel recommendations – the places they queue for, dream about on the flight home, and insist their friends book now.

From tiny sandwich counters in Florence to yakitori bars in Tokyo and natural wine spots in Lisbon, this is a living, breathing list of real food lovers’ favourite restaurants, bakeries, bars and markets around the world.

Use it to plan your next food trip, bolt a great meal onto a work trip, or just daydream between bookings.

How to Use This Guide

  • Pick your country or city and scan for the names that keep popping up in our community chats.
  • Expect a mix of high-end tasting menus, local institutions, street food, wine bars, bakeries and coffee spots.
  • Everything here is community-recommended – nothing sponsored, no PR briefs, just places our people genuinely loved.
